Over-the-air device firmware update (OTA-DFU)¶

The device provides you with several options for upgrading its firmware.

To perform a device firmware update (DFU) with custom firmware, you must first generate a DFU package. See Preparing for OTA-DFU.

Then, update the firmware by following the instructions in OTA-DFU with nRF Connect.
